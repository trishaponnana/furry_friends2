ABOUT US


Welcome to Paws and Home, where every dog finds a loving home!


At Paws and Home, our mission is to connect loving families with dogs in need of a new home. We understand that circumstances can change, and sometimes rehoming a beloved pet is the most compassionate choice. Our goal is to make this process as smooth and stress-free as possible for both the dogs and the families involved. We are a dedicated team of dog enthusiasts and animal welfare advocates who believe that every dog deserves a chance to be part of a loving family. Our team includes experienced pet caregivers, veterinarians, and volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure the well-being of every dog that comes through our doors.
